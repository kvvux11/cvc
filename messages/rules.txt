# Rules

1. Do not dox, threaten, swat, leak, or post personal information.
2. Do not raid, nuke, mass ping, or spam.
3. Do not post illegal content.
4. Do not post gore, NSFW, or weird content in public channels.
5. Do not impersonate staff or act like you have authority.
6. Do not scam, fake proof, fake payments, or lie in tickets.
7. Keep arguments out of main chat. Staff can move or end it.
8. Do not abuse private channels, special roles, or paid perks.
9. Do not beg for staff, roles, money, or special treatment.
10. Staff decisions are final. If there is a real issue, make a ticket.

Dark server does not mean stupid server. Keep it clean.
