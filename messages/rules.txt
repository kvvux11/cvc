# Rules

1. Do not leak personal information.
No doxxing, threats, swatting, leaking addresses, phone numbers, family info, schools, workplaces, or private accounts.

2. Do not raid or organize raids.
Do not use this server to target other servers, people, or communities.

3. Keep it controlled.
Dark humor and edgy jokes do not mean you can be racist, weird, or constantly start problems.

4. No scams.
Do not fake proof, fake payments, steal accounts, or trick members.

5. No spam.
Do not flood channels, spam pings, spam media, or abuse bot commands.

6. No NSFW.
Keep NSFW out of the server.

7. Respect staff decisions.
If staff tell you to stop, stop. If you disagree, open a ticket.

8. No impersonation.
Do not pretend to be staff, the owner, or another member.

9. Use channels properly.
Keep media in media channels, support in tickets, and normal chat in main channels.

10. Staff can act if needed.
Trying to loophole rules can still get you muted, kicked, banned, or restricted.
