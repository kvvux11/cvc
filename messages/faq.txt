# FAQ

## What is /ritual?
/ritual is a dark community server for chatting, media, pfps, banners, calls, events, giveaways, levels, and custom bot features.

## How do I unlock better roles?
Chat, be active, and level up.

Level rewards:
Level 10 → Verified
Level 20 → Trusted
Level 40 → Known
Level 50 → Proven
Level 100 → Elite
Level 1000 → Ascendant

## How do I speak in the ritual channel?
You need Trusted or higher. Trusted unlocks at Level 20.

## Can I buy roles?
Yes. Obsidian and Ascendant are special paid roles. Ascendant can also be earned free at Level 1000.

## What is Obsidian?
Obsidian is the highest paid status role. It is not earned by leveling.

## What is Ascendant?
Ascendant is a special status role. You can buy it or earn it free at Level 1000.

## How do I buy special roles?
Open a ticket and wait for the owner to respond. Payments are handled by the owner only.
