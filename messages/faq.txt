# FAQ

## What is /ritual?
/ritual is a dark community server built around chat, media, voice, giveaways, events, profiles, banners, cars, animals, and a custom bot.

## What is skid?
skid is the server bot. It handles tickets, levels, logs, applications, voice channels, commands, and auto media posting.

## How do I level up?
Chat normally and join active voice calls. Spam does not help.

## How do I get special roles?
Open a ticket and ask about special roles. Obsidian, Ascendant, custom roles, and private channels are handled through owner-approved payments.

## Can I apply for staff?
Yes. Use the application panel. The bot will DM you the questions one by one.

## Why did my application get denied automatically?
Your answer was too short, fake, or low effort. Reapply when you are ready to answer properly.

## Can staff see private channels?
Yes. Private channels are private from members, not from staff.
