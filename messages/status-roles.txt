# ⌁ Special Roles

Special roles are optional paid perks for members who want extra access, status, or custom features inside /ritual.

Payments are handled through tickets only. Do not send money to random members.

## ⛧ Obsidian
Highest paid status role.

Price:
• $5 monthly
• $12 permanent

## ⟡ Ascendant
Paid status role, or earned free by reaching Level 1000.

Price:
• $3 monthly
• $8 permanent

## ⌁ Custom Role
Choose your own role name and color.

Price:
• $5 one-time

## ⌁ Private Channel
A private channel gives you your own text channel.

Price:
• $8 monthly
• $18 permanent

## ⌁ Custom Bundle
Custom role + private channel.

Price:
• $20 permanent
