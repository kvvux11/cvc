# Special Roles

Special roles are optional paid perks for members who want higher placement, private access, or custom features.

## ⛧ Obsidian
Highest special role.

Includes:
• private Obsidian chat
• higher role placement
• priority support
• special role color
• access to selected perks

## ⟡ Ascendant
Lower special role.

Includes:
• Ascendant chat
• better role placement
• priority support
• selected perks

## Custom Role
Pick your own role name and color.

Rules:
• no staff names
• no impersonation
• no slurs
• no dox/threat names
• staff can deny anything that causes issues

## Private Channel
Your own channel inside the private category.

You can:
• change the channel name/topic
• invite/remove people
• send files and links
• manage your own channel

You cannot:
• hide it from staff
• post illegal content
• dox/threaten/leak
• abuse permissions

## How to buy
Open a ticket and select Special Roles.
Only pay through the owner or approved Stripe link.
