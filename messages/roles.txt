# ⛧ Roles

⌁ Initiate
Default member role.

⟐ Verified
Unlocked at Level 10.

⟡ Trusted
Unlocked at Level 20. Trusted members can chat in the ritual channel.

⟠ Known
Unlocked at Level 40.

⛧ Proven
Unlocked at Level 50.

♱ Elite
Unlocked at Level 100.

⟡ Ascendant
Paid status role, or earned free at Level 1000.

⛧ Obsidian
Highest paid status role.

✟ Founder
Server owner.

⟡ Warden
Administrator.

⟠ Enforcer
Moderator.

⌁ Trial
Trial moderator.

✦ Support
Ticket support.
