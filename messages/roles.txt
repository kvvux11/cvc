# Roles

✟ Founder
Server owner.

⟡ Warden
High staff. Handles serious issues, bans, staff decisions, and server control.

⟠ Enforcer
Moderator. Handles tickets, reports, timeouts, kicks, chat control, and rule breaking.

⌁ Trial
Trial staff with limited perms. Mainly timeouts, clears, tickets, and basic moderation.

✦ Support
Ticket/support helper. Helps members but does not have full mod power.

⛧ Staff
General staff access role. This alone does not mean full moderation power.

⛧ Obsidian
Highest special role. Higher placement, private chat access, and better priority.

⟡ Ascendant
Lower special role. Better placement and priority without full Obsidian perks.

♱ Booster
Server booster role.

✦ Elite / ⛧ Proven / ⟠ Known / ⟡ Trusted / ⟐ Verified / ⌁ Initiate
Member status roles earned through activity, trust, or staff approval.

⌁ Level 10 / ⟐ Level 20 / ⟡ Level 40 / ⟠ Level 50 / ⛧ Level 100 / ♱ Level 1000
Level roles earned from chatting and VC activity.

⟐ Giveaway Winner
Given to giveaway winners.

PC / Xbox / Playstation
Platform roles.

Media / Events / Giveaways
Ping roles for people who want those alerts.
